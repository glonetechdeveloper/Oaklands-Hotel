Our Park | Diner | Bar | Gym & Conference rooms

Discover the Park Views

Nestled in the heart of nature, our hotel offers stunning park views that create the perfect balance of relaxation and inspiration. Take in the lush greenery, the calm breeze, and a peaceful environment right from your window.

Conference Hall

Our modern conference hall is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, making it the ideal venue for business meetings, events, and gatherings. Designed with comfort and professionalism in mind, it provides the perfect balance between elegance and functionality.

Flavors That Tell a Story

Indulge in a dining experience that blends timeless elegance with local flavor. Our diner and bar offer an inviting atmosphere, where every meal and cocktail carries the story of The Oaklands.

Stay Active, Stay Inspired

Our state-of-the-art gym is designed to keep you energized throughout your stay. Featuring modern equipment, open space for functional training, and a calm atmosphere overlooking the park, it’s the perfect place to refresh your body and mind.
